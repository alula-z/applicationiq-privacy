Privacy Policy

ApplicationIQ  ·  Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ApplicationIQ ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our iOS application. By using ApplicationIQ, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide the app's features.

Google Account & Gmail Access
When you choose to connect your Gmail account, we request read-only access to your Gmail inbox via Google Sign-In. We use this access solely to detect job application-related emails (such as application confirmations, interview invitations, and rejections). We do not read, store, or share any other emails. Your Gmail email address is stored locally on your device to display your connected account.

Email Content (Processed, Not Stored)
To classify job-related emails, the content of relevant emails is temporarily sent to our AI classification service (powered by Anthropic's Claude API via a secure proxy). Email content is used only for classification and is not stored on any server after processing.

Subscription & Purchase Information
We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase and subscription data. Actual payment processing is handled entirely by Apple — we never see or store your payment details.

App Data
All job application data you create or import (company names, roles, statuses, notes) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not have access to this data.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your full name
  • Phone number
  • Physical address
  • Social media accounts
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for advertising
  • Crash reports or usage analytics

We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase, or any third-party analytics or advertising tools.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely to:

  • Authenticate your Google account for Gmail access
  • Detect and import job application emails from your inbox
  • Manage your Pro subscription status
  • Display your connected Gmail address within the app

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

ApplicationIQ uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In — for Gmail OAuth authentication. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — for subscription management. Subject to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic Claude API — for AI-powered email classification via a secure proxy. Email content is processed and not stored. Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple — for payment processing and app distribution. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage & Security

All of your job application data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal data. Email content passed to our classification service is transmitted over HTTPS and is not retained after processing.

Deleting the ApplicationIQ app from your device will permanently remove all locally stored data.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access — you can request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Right to erasure — you can request deletion of your data at any time
  • Right to rectification — you can request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to restrict processing — you can request we limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — you can request your data in a portable format
  • Right to object — you can object to our processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below. Since all app data is stored locally on your device, deleting the app is the most effective way to erase your data immediately.

7. Children's Privacy

ApplicationIQ is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
